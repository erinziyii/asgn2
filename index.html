World Happiness Report(2015-2022) :)

A visualisation analysis of the happiness of individuals around the world and the factors which affect it.

Happiness Map Representation

The map below shows the global changes in happiness scores from the year 2015 to 2022. With further analysis of the map, we can observe that the happiest countries in the world are mostly found in Europe and Oceania, whereas the countries with the lowest happiness scores are mostly found in Asia and Africa.

Happiness Factors

The 6 factors which affect the happiness scores of each country include: health, family, economy, generosity, government trust, and freedom. Overall, we can observe from the scatter plots below that countries in Europe(green), Oceania(brown), and North America(blue) have a higher happiness score as compared to South America(orange), Asia(red), and especially Africa(purple) which is placed the lowest in happiness
